Papla

Born from a deep concern for plastic pollution, Papla was founded in 2018 by Saranya and Devakumar to offer a sustainable alternative. Each of our palm leaf plates is handcrafted from naturally fallen areca leaves, combining elegance with eco-friendliness. Papla is more than tableware – it’s a movement to bring nature back to your table.
