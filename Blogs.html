PadCare Labs

The Founder Ajinkya Dhariya of PadCare Labs Has Managed to Protect Environment With his Innovation . In India, around 1200 crores sanitary napkins are generated every year, out of which 98% go into landfills and water bodies. in absence of dognified way to dispose sanitary napkins, Padcare was born.
